🌌 Fact 1: There are more possible chess games than atoms in the observable universe

The number of possible unique chess games is estimated to be around 10^120 (called the Shannon Number).

How can a simple game create such a huge number?

Because every move creates new possibilities. At the start of a chess game, White has 20 legal moves Black then has about 20 possible responses. After just two moves by each player, there are already thousands of possible positions.

🦈Fact 2: Sharks are older than trees

The earliest sharks appeared about 400 million years ago

How old are sharks?

The first trees appeared around 350 million years ago.So sharks were already swimming in the oceans roughly 50 million years before the first tree existed on Earth.

So where were the trees?

Before trees existed, land looked very different. Around 400 million years ago, land was mostly covered with moss like small plants, small primitive vegetation and bare rock and soil

🍌 Fact 3: Bananas are berries, but strawberries aren't

In everyday language, a berry is just a small, juicy fruit. But in botany a berry has a very specific definition. A true berry must develop one flower, one ovary and have seeds embedded inside the fleshy fruit

Why IS a banana a berry?

A banana develops from a single flower with a single ovary. Its seeds are contained within the fleshy fruit. That perfectly matches the botanical definition of a berry.
